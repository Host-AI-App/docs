## Overview

Conduit doesn't offer a dedicated mobile app, but users can easily access it on-the-go by bookmarking the webpage directly to their device's home screen. This enables support handling from anywhere, keeping users connected with guests even away from their desk.

## Setup Instructions

### iPhone

1. Open Safari and navigate to https://base.conduit.ai/
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the navigation bar
3. Select **Add to Home Screen**
4. Conduit will appear on your home screen like an app for easy access

### Android

1. Open your mobile browser and go to https://base.conduit.ai/
2. Tap the **Menu** button (three dots) and select **Add to Home Screen**
3. Conduit will be added to your home screen for quick access

Alternatively, follow [Google's support documentation](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/9658361?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid) for detailed instructions.

<Note>
Desktop clients are recommended for the best experience.
</Note>
