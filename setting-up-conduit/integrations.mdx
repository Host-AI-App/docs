## Overview

Conduit enables connections to Google Drive and Notion pages, allowing users to manage files, documents, and folders directly within Conduit.

## Setup Instructions

To connect Notion and Google Drive, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations** > **Discover**
2. Select the external platform you would like to integrate
3. Allow Conduit to have access to these pages
