## Overview

WhatsApp Business with Conduit enables users to **receive calls** in the web application. **Making outbound calls is not currently supported.**

## Meta Business Setup

Start receiving calls by following these steps:

### 1. Go to Meta Business

Visit the Meta Business settings page at business.facebook.com

### 2. Visit "WhatsApp accounts"

Navigate to the WhatsApp accounts section in your Meta Business settings.

### 3. Visit WhatsApp Manager

Locate your WhatsApp Business Account and access WhatsApp Manager.

### 4. Enable Calls

In WhatsApp Manager:

- Select your Phone Number
- Within your Phone Number settings, select "calls"

<Warning>
You must already have this WhatsApp number connected to Conduit. If not, the "Allow voice calls" option cannot be turned on.
</Warning>
